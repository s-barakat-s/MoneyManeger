# Money Manager SaaS - Future Roadmap (Post V1)

The core financial engine should be completed first.

The goal is to finish the business logic before investing time in UI/UX improvements.

---

# Phase 1 (Core)

✅ Owners

✅ Transactions

✅ Transfers

✅ Debts

✅ Receivables

✅ Company Assets

✅ Dynamic Balance Calculation

✅ Dashboard

---

# Phase 2 (Reports & Productivity)

## Financial Reports

Create a dedicated Reports section.

Reports should support:

* Income Report
* Expense Report
* Cash Flow
* Receivables Report
* Debts Report
* Assets Report
* Profit Summary

Filtering:

* Today
* This Week
* This Month
* This Year
* Custom Date Range

Export:

* PDF
* Excel

---

## Search

Global search across the entire application.

Examples:

Searching for:

Ahmed

Should return:

* Owner
* Transactions
* Transfers
* Debts
* Receivables
* Assets

---

## Advanced Filters

Every page should support filtering.

Transactions:

* Owner
* Income
* Expense
* Date
* Amount

Debts:

* Open
* Paid
* Partial

Receivables:

* Open
* Collected
* Partial

Assets:

* Category
* Purchase Date

---

# Phase 3 (Organization)

## Categories

Transactions should support categories.

Expense Categories:

* Rent
* Salaries
* Shipping
* Packaging
* Ads
* Fabric
* Utilities
* Taxes
* Other

Income Categories:

* Sales
* Investment
* Refund
* Other

These categories will improve reporting significantly.

---

## Attachments

Allow attaching:

* Images
* PDFs
* Receipts
* Invoices

To:

* Transactions
* Debts
* Receivables
* Assets

---

## Due Dates

Support due dates for:

* Debts
* Receivables

Show overdue items automatically.

---

## Partial Payment History

Instead of only showing total paid,

Display every payment separately with:

* Amount
* Date
* Note

---

# Phase 4 (Safety & History)

## Activity Log

Track every important action.

Examples:

* Owner created
* Transaction edited
* Transfer deleted
* Debt paid
* Asset added

Display:

User

Action

Date

Time

---

## Archive

Instead of deleting important data,

Allow:

* Archive Owner
* Archive Asset
* Archive Debt
* Archive Receivable

---

## Trash

Recover accidentally deleted items.

---

## Undo Actions

After deleting an item,

Allow Undo for a few seconds.

---

# Phase 5 (Business Management)

## Capital Management

Separate capital movements from normal business operations.

Support:

Owner Contribution

Owner Withdrawal

Track:

* Total company capital
* Capital per owner
* Contribution history
* Withdrawal history

These operations must NOT be treated as Income or Expense.

---

## Notifications

Examples:

* Debt overdue
* Receivable overdue
* Large expense
* Large transfer
* Low available cash

---

## Financial Health Engine

Automatically analyze company status.

Statuses:

* Healthy
* Warning
* Critical

Based on:

* Cash
* Debts
* Receivables

Provide simple financial insights.

* Smooth animations
* Responsive design
* Desktop-first experience
* Mobile optimization
* Better spacing
* Modern typography
* Better color system
* Empty states
* Skeleton loaders
* Beautiful dashboard
* Interactive charts
* Better dialogs
* Improved navigation
* Consistent design language

The UI redesign should be done only after the business logic is fully completed.
